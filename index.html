Her Sweet & Goofy Side

Think you know her? Think again. It's time for you to embrace Her Sweet & Goofy Side. A love letter that unravels the charm and danger of the femme fatale. A reflection on the magnetic yet unpredictable power that she holds, blending sweetness with mischief whose charm is both intoxicating and dangerous. She’s the protagonist, the villain, the muse and the mastermind. The one you can't forget, and now she has a soundtrack. Not everything sweet is safe. But you can't help yourself as you click play and let her take you with her.
